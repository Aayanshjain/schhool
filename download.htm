Modern School, Barakhamba Road

Gender: Coeducational
Type: Day and boarding school
Age range: x - x

Established in 1920, Modern school epitomizes the ideals of its founding father and is synonymous with a perfect synchronization of modern education with Indian ethos.  Consistently ranked among the best, Modern school believes in rendering holistic education and motivates the students to go beyond the text book learning and becoming informed citizens of the world.

The IDEALS in our school

The ideals of Round Square are embedded in the very ethos of Modern School. The various activities, symposia, workshops, trips and exchange visits are all avenues for the children to explore the various ideals of service, adventure, democracy, internationalism besides others. Being an oasis in the concrete jungle, through its very active Nature Club, the students at Modern practise good green deeds and boast of solar panels, rainwater harvesting, vermi-composting besides regular  tree plantation drives. A consistent and collective effort, “to strive, to seek, to find, and not to yield”, is the objective of the school as mentioned by Sir Alfred Lord Tennyson.

What being a Round Square school means to us

Round Square opens a world of opportunity exploring new avenues for the like minded-young future leaders. Striving for ‘one-world’ in the time of increasing polarization, racialism and cultural barriers, is its motto.

Round Square has ignited the young minds at Modern School focusing at a students’ platform for nurturing the ideals of community service, human rights and sustainable development. Round Square, effectively motivates each one to look beyond individualism and embrace internationalism to become a world citizen.
